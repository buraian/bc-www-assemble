iMPACT Shelf

The iMPACT Shelf app was developed to be your "bookshelf" on the iPad. Inside the iMPACT Shelf, you'll find additional apps, iMPACT training content, competitive intelligence information, product launch videos and important and timely information from home office departments to recruiters. All iMPACT Shelf apps and content are for internal use only by Pacific Life recruiters.

Competitive Intelligence Playbook

Once you have downloaded and installed the iMPACT Shelf app onto your new iPad, you will find a new Competitive Intelligence Playbook app that resides in the iMPACT Shelf. This CI Playbook app has been developed by Paul Ligeros and John Church to deliver timely and relevant competitive information to your fingertips (literally)! Updated quarterly, the new CI Playbook app is for internal use only and will be a continual source of competitive intelligence on 18 competitors. So, download it and install it from the "Download Library" tab inside the iMPACT Shelf.
